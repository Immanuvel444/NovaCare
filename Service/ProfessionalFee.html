Overview

Our diverse team of professional fee (ProFee) coders receives detailed, specialty-specific training to ensure exceptional quality and improved clean claim submission rates. The Novah Care implementation process is seamless and includes effective controls to ensure minimal disruption – all with 3-4x return on investment (ROI).
