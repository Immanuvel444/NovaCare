Facility Coding Services

Avoid coding-related denials, reduce days in A/R, and improve revenue capture across all facility types.

Connect with us

Overview

Our experienced team of college-educated coders receives extensive training and continuous education to ensure accurate reimbursements across all types of facility coding environments, including inpatient, outpatient, and emergency department settings. Quality and compliance are supported by a dedicated compliance task force, regular auditing, robust quality management controls, and custom workflow tools to ensure coding accuracy and diligent coding compliance at all levels.

We maintain an active line of communication with your team with respect to documentation, providing actionable recommendations that will eliminate claim denials. Our customers also receive an extensive suite of reports and high-impact analyses, including thorough investigations of clinical documentation integrity (CDI) mismatches, physician queries, MS-DRG vs. APR-DRG, GM-LOS vs. actual LOS, and more.

SERVICES PROVIDED

Expert coders and dedicated coding software and workflow tools ensure coding accuracy and high productivity for a variety of departments including:
  • Inpatient
  • Emergency department
  • Same-day surgery
  • Observations
  • Urgent care
  • Ancillaries

SERVICES PROVIDED

Our highly trained staff can assist with the coding of complex cases for risk adjustment, such as HCC coding, to ensure accurate RAF scoring and reimbursements among patients with chronic conditions.
Novah Care will:
  • Conduct high-accuracy diagnostic coding.
  • Ensure medical records are complete and accurate based on Hierarchical Condition Categories (HCCs) guidelines.
  • Verify risk-adjusted codes and documentation based on industry standards.
  • Provide assessment of documentation based on M.E.A.T. criteria.
  • Supply dashboards and analytical tools to help you manage your Risk Adjustment population.
