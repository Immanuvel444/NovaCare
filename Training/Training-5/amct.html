Advanced Medical Coding Training

(AMCT)

Our course is designed to provide a comprehensive understanding of medical coding principles, preparing you for the CPC certification exam administered by the American Academy of Professional Coders (AAPC). This program integrates essential coding skills with advanced training required for CPC certification.

  • Expert Trainers
  • Online / Classroom Training
  • Certified Course



Overview


Course Offerings


Our course offers foundational skills in medical terminology, anatomy, and basic ICD-10-CM coding. It also includes advanced training in complex ICD-10-CM and CPT coding, HCPCS Level II coding, and compliance with coding guidelines. You'll receive thorough preparation for the CPC certification exam, equipping you for roles in medical coding departments in Healthcare facilities, and Insurance companies.


Vital Features

  •  Basics of Human Anatomy
  •  Medical Terminology (Anatomy, Physiology, Pathology, Laboratory Techniques, etc.)
  •  HIPAA Compliance

  •  ICD-10-CM (International Classification of Diseases, 10th Edition, Clinical Modification)
  •  CPT (Current Procedural Terminology) Coding
  •  PCS Coding
  •  HCPCS (Healthcare Common Procedure Coding System) Coding
  •  Certification oriented training
  •  Mock Assessment

Course Details


Learning content


  •  Language of Medicines (Medical Terminology, Anatomy, Physiology, Pathology, Laboratory, Diagnosis procedures etc.)
  •  Introduction to Revenue Cycle Management
  •  ICD-10-CM Coding Conventions and Chapter specific guidelines
  •  Introduction to CPT Procedure Coding Terminologies
  •  Introduction on HIPAA rules and guidelines
  •  Revenue Cycle Management
  •  All sections (i.e.) E&M (Evaluation and Management), Surgery, Anesthesia, Radiology, Medical Billing
  •  The combined basic medical coding and CPC course offers a comprehensive educational experience, blending foundational knowledge with advanced CPC preparation. It prepares participants not only for entry-level coding positions but also for the CPC certification exam, setting them on a path towards a successful career in Novahcare coding and billing.
